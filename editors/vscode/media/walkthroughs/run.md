## Run your first form

Frothy runs *forms* — complete definitions or expressions — live on the
device.

- Put the cursor inside a form and press <kbd>Cmd/Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>Enter</kbd>,
  or click the **▷ Run** lens above it.
- <kbd>Cmd/Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>Shift</kbd>+<kbd>Enter</kbd> runs the whole file.
- <kbd>Cmd/Ctrl</kbd>+<kbd>Alt</kbd>+<kbd>.</kbd> interrupts a running form.

The device's replies appear in the Frothy output channel; compile errors mark
the offending form in the Problems panel.

**Frothy: Open Example** gives you working sketches to start from.
